    Annual Christmas Parade on for 2021

    The Fish Haven annual Christmas parade is back on after a brief hiatus during the year 2020. Expect to see the high school marching band and dance crew, the Boy Scouts of America, and a special float featuring the big guy himself, Santa, along with Mrs Clause, Rudolph, and several happy elves. The parade will start on 5th street at 9:00 am and proceed down main and end on Newberry Road. The Fish Haven Christmas Parade is a 78 year long tradition, starting in 1943 and continuing on uninterrupted until the Covid-19 pandemic forced a small hiatus in its operation. Mayor Gustov Jonas expressed his delight about the return of the parade saying “The Christmas parade is an important part of our city’s Christmas Celebrations. We are thrilled to have it back, and we express gratitude to the many hard working people who made this possible.”
