Local Flower Growers Share Craft in New Class

The Preston Flower Growers Association is advertising a new workshop starting this winter. The Preston Flower Growers Association has teamed up with the Idaho state extension to provide a flower centric class every Friday throughout January and February at the Happy Florist Flower Shop on Main Street. Classes include garden planning, perennial care, table flower arrangements, and many more. Ann Martin, president of the Preston Flower Growers Association, said that she “couldn’t be happier about the opportunity to bring the joy of flowers to the citizens of Preston.” She also extended her thanks to the Idaho State Extension for providing teachers and resources. For more information or to sign up visit www.NotARealWebsite.com
