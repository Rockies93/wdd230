Petition to Light up the Geyser Gains Steam

Earlier this year an interesting petition began circling around the city of Soda Springs. Certain individuals want to install color changing lights by the Soda Springs Geyser to illuminate the stream of water when released. Jerry Jefferson, who heads the movement said to Rain or Shine, “Think about how cool it would be. We could have it turn red or green for Christmas, pink for Easter, or Orange for Halloween.” Soda Springs is the only man made geyser in existence and came about when entrepreneurial citizens accidentally drilled into a pressurized chamber, unleashing the geyser. It is currently electronically controlled and goes off every hour on the hour. Jefferson concluded his statement with: “Come on guys, why aren’t we doing this?” Why not indeed?
