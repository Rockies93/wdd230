Site Purpose

Rain or Shine Weather service is built to provide weather data to the southern Idaho area. It will provide predicted weather events, high and low temperatures, a daily weather timeline, and a comparison of projected temperatures to average temperatures. We also hope to provide informative content that is relative to the community, such as information on current weather patterns, or relevant local news.
