Target Audience

The target audience of Sunny Day Weather is the adult residents of the _____ area. This includes those who need to know the weather for personal or professional reasons. We hope to make weather information easily accessible to both those who are comfortable with the web and those who are uncomfortable with it.

Sally Jenkins

Job Title: Retired

Demographics: 69 Years old, married 45 years, mother to 3 children, proud grandmother, avid Gardener

Goals: She needs to stay up to date on weather patterns in order to take care of her garden. She also likes to know if she needs an umbrella on her morning walk or if its a good day to take the grandkids to the park.

Environment: Sally is an intermittent technology user. While not the most well versed in technology, she feels mostly comfortable using the internet. She accesses Sunny Days Weather via desktop.

Scenarios

  • Will there be a frost tonight? Should I cover my tomatoes?
  • Should I adjust my sprinkler system to account for rain?
  • Will it be warm enough to walk to little Johnny’s school play?
  • I think I’ll knit Susan a new hat. When should I have it ready?
  • It’s been rather hot lately. Is it hot enough that my greens will bolt? Should I use the sun shade?

Josh Smithson

Job Title: Landscape Specialist

Demographics: 29 years old, married 3 years, has a 1-year-old baby, studied Horticulture in college, has been working for ____ landscape company for 5 years.

Josh oversees 7-10 landscape crews. He regularly checks the weather while making or revising service schedules. Most of his traffic would come over a cell phone or tablet. Occasionally he accesses the site on his desktop.

Environment: Josh is a competent technology user. He comfortably surfs the web for both professional and personal reasons. He feels comfortable finding reliable sources on the internet.

Scenarios

  • How much rain is expected Tuesday? Is it enough that I should shuffle the service schedule to allow for a few hours of heavy rain?
  • Will I need to send out snow removal crews this week? When?
  • Is it safe to spray pesticides today?
  • Will it be too hot to safely work in the afternoon?
  • What is the weather on Thursday? Can I schedule the sod delivery for that day?
